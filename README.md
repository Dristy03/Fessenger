There is nothing more precious than reading something you wrote to be delivered to your self in the future! Think how nostalgic you will be getting to know your past self or delighted to see succeeding some goals you have set in the past or amazed to see how different you were back then when you wrote it.

Fessenger brings you this precious opportunity at your hand. You can write to yourself a mail to be sent any day in the future whether tomorrow or 20 years later. Your mail will always arrive at your inbox on the day you choose to receive it.
